VOC3 metric: Ontology documentation: all terms have labels

This metric evaluates the extent to which all ontology terms have a human readable label.

What is being measured?

The presence of human-readable labels for all ontology terms.

Why should we measure it?

Having labels for ontology terms improves usability and understanding, making ontologies easier to work with for humans and systems.

What must be provided for the measurement?

An ontology URI or file.

What are considered valid results?

All ontology terms have labels defined.
